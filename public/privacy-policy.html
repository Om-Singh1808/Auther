Data Deletion Instructions for Auther

Last updated: August 21, 2024

How to Delete Your Data

To delete your data from Auther, follow these steps:

  1. Log into your Auther account at https://auther-fwday33i7-oms-projects-db41c888.vercel.app
  2. Go to your profile settings
  3. Click "Delete Account" or "Remove Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Alternative Methods

If you cannot access your account, you can also:

  • Email us: privacy@auther.com
  • Contact us: Include your Facebook user ID and request data deletion

What Data We Delete

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your account information
  • Your Facebook access tokens
  • Any posts you created through our app
  • All associated data

Confirmation

After processing your deletion request, you will receive a confirmation email. The deletion process may take up to 30 days to complete.

Contact Information

Email: privacy@auther.com

Response Time: We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days.
